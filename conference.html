Industry Leaders Committed to Ethical Investing

Ayub Kayat

Founder, Ethical Wealth Academy

With over 20 years of property industry experience, Ayub specializes in social housing and high-yield strategies attainable through prudent planning and minimal borrowing. He'll share actionable strategies on portfolio growth while maintaining ethical principles, and discuss the importance of the right mindset in overcoming fear, limiting beliefs, and mental blocks.

Saj Hussain

Property Expert & Industry Speaker

A recognized property expert who will illuminate current market trends, portfolio growth tactics, and practical approaches to scaling investments. Saj brings real-world insights into navigating today's property landscape with confidence and strategy.

Shaikh Zaheer Mahmood

Founder, Al Suffa Institute Birmingham

An internationally renowned speaker who has traveled from Barbados to Malaysia, reviving hearts with Islamic knowledge. Shaikh Zaheer's unique delivery style radiates zeal and candour. As founder of Al Suffa Institute in Birmingham, he has transformed countless young people with Sharia knowledge emphasizing ethical business practices.

Looay Gharib

CEO, Kubix - Shopify's UK Premier Partner

Ten years ago, Looay began his first chapter in West Yorkshire, where he built meaningful connections that shaped his career. One of those early relationships was with Ayub, his very first client, who introduced him to trusted contacts that continue to this day and helped spark Kubix.

Today, Kubix stands as a powerhouse in eCommerce across Europe, partnering with major global brands. As Klaviyo Platinum Masters and Google & Meta Partners, Looay brings a decade of experience in growth, investment, and business transformation. His expertise in digital marketing, commerce, and strategic scaling is built on a deep belief in the power of trust and relationships to fuel long-term success.

Together, these speakers provide a well-rounded program that equips attendees with proven methods, real-world case studies, and the motivation to pursue ambitious financial goals responsibly.
